Based in Seoul, South Korea, 'Wave to Earth' is a three-member band consisting of Daniel Kim, drummer Dong Kyu Shin, and bassist John Cha.

'Wave to Earth' produces indie pop with a lo-fi sound based on jazz drumming, creating a unique sense of the seasons and a dreamy atmosphere in the music.

With the primary motto of "All Self-made," all recording, mixing, and mastering are done directly by the band members, who also direct the overall art field, such as album art, video, and fashion.
